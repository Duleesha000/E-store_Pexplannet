The Story




In The Beginning

Simplytek Was Launched In 2017, But Its Story Actually Began A Couple Of Years Before That When A Group Of Friends Decided To Go Into Business Together. We Started Small, But Our Combined Ambition, Drive And Abilities Soon Made Us Look For New Challenges And Enter New Markets, Whereas Starting Our Own Store Enabled Us To Develop A Strong Presence In The Mobile Phone Industry.

Throughout The Years We Have Spent In The Business We Have Developed A Vast Network Of Loyal, And Ever Expanding Base Of Customers From All Over The Island. Combined With Our Team’s Collective Experience Of Superior Customer Service, And Our Research And Development Team’s Regular Exploration Of New Product Ranges, We Have Branded Ourselves In The Market As A One-stop Consumer Electronics Store For Any Requirement. Our Affordability, Fast And Reliable Delivery, And The Fact That You Will Always Be Able To Find The Product That You Are Looking For In Our Store, Has Made Us Stand Out In The Market, But These Are Simply Our Objectives And Dedication Towards Constantly Improving Our Services. In Order To Achieve Our Goals, We Know That We Need To Keep In Close Touch With Our Customers, Listen To Their Suggestions, And Learn From Their Critique. This Is Why Our Customer Service Is Always Ready To Answer Any Question That You May Have, And Is Just As Willing To Listen, As It Is To Inform.
